1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Email address and authentication credentials (only during login/logout).
  • Usage Data: Timestamps and playback activity of meditation audio files.
  • Device Information: Basic diagnostic and performance metrics (e.g., device type, OS version).

No biometric or location data is collected.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate access and distinguish between admin/general users.
  • Track audio playback for analytical and admin visibility.
  • Improve app functionality and provide a personalized experience.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is securely stored using Google Firebase, which provides encrypted cloud-based storage and database solutions.
  • Audio files may be downloaded locally on your device for offline playback.
  • We implement safeguards to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Admin Access

  • The admin user can upload meditation audio files.
  • The admin user can view playback history (user ID + timestamp) for each audio file.
  • The admin user cannot access passwords or sensitive credentials.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except:

  • When required by law or legal processes.
  • To enforce terms and protect our rights.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our app does not use cookies or external trackers. Firebase may store session-based identifiers to manage authentication and app behavior.
